Rules and Regulation

Graphic designing


Article 1: Subject

There are 4 competitions as of now: Graphic Designing, Photography, Quiz and Content writing. The quiz consists of a multiple choice questionnaire on a particular topic that will be posted on our Facebook page & Website. The winners will be chosen by our judges from the names of the participants who have realized a perfect score. For graphic designing, content writing and photography the topics will be posted same as for the quiz.
Registration deadlines for Graphic Designing: 17th May 2020

Article 2: Conditions of participation

  • 2.1 Participation in the competitions is free and open to all persons above 16 years old.

  • 2.2 Big-Path reserves the right to conduct necessary checks in order to verify the identity and/or e-mail address of the participants.

  • 2.3 The persons who have given incomplete or inaccurate personal and contact details will be disqualified for the draw, as will be the persons who refuse that their personal details – which are necessary for the game – be collected, kept and used.

  • 2.4 Participation in the competitions implies that participants accept the integrality of these rules. Non-compliance with the rules results in the automatic cancellation of the participation in the competitions and of the candidacy for the prize

Article 3: Terms of participation

  • 3.1 The topic for Graphic Designing will be posted on our Website & Facebook page on 18th May 2020.

  • 3.2 Graphic Designing participants will have to submit their source file along with their final project on the email big.path@outlook.com For Graphic Designing the best 3 candidates will be chosen and their projects will be posted on our Facebook page for Public Polling.

  • 3.3 Public Polling will start from 23rd May 2020 and will long up to 25th May 2020.

  • 3.4 Winner will be selected 40% based on Public Polling & 60% by our Judges.

  • 3.5 The final submission date 22nd May 2020 (12:00PM).

  • 3.6 Most Important: Late submission exceeding even a minute will not be entertained.

Article 4: Awarding the prize

  • 4.1 The winners will be decided entirely on merit and contacted via e-mail (on 25th May 2020). The prize money will be sent to their Easypaisa or Jazzcash account as per the information provided on their registration form. Winners of each competition will receive cash prize of PKR 1000.

Article 5: Publication of the results

  • All participants will be able to see the overall results of the competitions on:
  • Big-Path website
  • Big-Path facebook page
  • Big-Path instagram page
